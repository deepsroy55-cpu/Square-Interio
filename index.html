Who We Are

Designing Spaces That Reflect Your Lifestyle

At Square Interio, we believe that the spaces we inhabit have a profound impact on our well-being. We don't design for fleeting trends; we design for the people who live in them. Our spaces blend aesthetics with functionality, giving you a civilized, beautiful, and highly personalized home.

Based in Patna (Bihar) and Ranchi (Jharkhand), we specialize in premium home interior designs, modern modular kitchens, custom wardrobes, elegant drawing halls, luxury bathroom remodeling, and premium commercial office interiors across both states.

Our collaborative designer approach ensures every detail is custom built around your requirements, choice of premium materials, and space usability configurations.
