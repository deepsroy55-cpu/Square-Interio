Modular Kitchen Cost in Patna (2026 Price Guide)

June 22, 2026 | By Square Interio

Are you planning to install a modern modular kitchen in your flat or duplex in Patna? One of the first questions you will ask is: "How much does a modular kitchen cost in Patna?"

The honest answer is that the cost depends on your selections—size of the kitchen, layout, core wood grade, shutter finish, and accessory brands. In this guide, we break down standard pricing variables in Patna, Bihar, to help you plan your budget.

1. Cost by Kitchen Size & Layout

Layout dictates structural cost. Straight kitchens are budget-friendly, while U-shape or Parallel islands cost more due to added granite counter lengths and double cabinetry:

  • Straight Kitchen (8ft - 10ft): ₹1.2 Lakhs to ₹1.8 Lakhs (ideal for small flats).
  • L-Shape Kitchen (Standard 2BHK/3BHK): ₹1.8 Lakhs to ₹3.2 Lakhs (most popular in Patna).
  • U-Shape Kitchen (Spacious layout): ₹3 Lakhs to ₹5 Lakhs (maximum storage).

2. Wood Grade & Core Materials

Never settle for cheap MDF or local Particle Board in high-humidity zones like kitchens. Patna experiences humid summers, meaning moisture resistant ply is mandatory:

  • BWR (Boiling Water Resistant) Plywood: The standard choice. Resists moisture and termites effectively.
  • BWP (Boiling Water Proof) Marine Plywood: 100% waterproof, suitable for the sink area cabinet.

3. Shutter Finishes

The visual finish determines the premium look. High-gloss Acrylic finishes look modern and reflect light well in dark rooms, but cost about 20-30% more than Matte/Glossy Laminates. Laminate is highly durable and budget-friendly.
