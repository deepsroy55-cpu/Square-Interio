10 Modern Drawing Hall Design Ideas for Patna Homes

June 22, 2026 | By Square Interio

The drawing hall is the primary guest zone of a home, setting the first impression of your styling choices. If you are building or renovating your apartment in Patna, Kankarbagh, Bailey Road, or Danapur, here are 10 expert drawing hall design trends:

1. Textured Louvered Back panels

Instead of plain paint, use charcoal louvers or fluted wood panels as a backdrop for your sofa console or display units. It adds immediate premium touch and depth.

2. Smart Jaali partitions

For open-plan 3BHK/4BHK flats, divide the drawing and dining spaces with a CNC cut wooden jaali or a fluted glass partition with gold metal trims. It preserves open space while creating privacy.

3. Warm LED Profile Lighting

Layering light is crucial. Combine false ceilings with warm profile light strips, cob spot lights, and elegant hanging pendants next to seating layouts.

4. Earthy Neutral Color Schemes

Warm beige, ivory, light taupe, and soft grey make the drawing room look spacious and calm. Use gold metal strips, dark veneers, or accent cushions to introduce highlights.

5. Backlit Marble TV Backdrops

A floating TV console table against a backlit white statuario PVC sheet or natural onyx stone backdrop makes your entertainment wall look futuristic and high-end.
