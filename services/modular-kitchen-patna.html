Modular Kitchen Design in Patna

Ergonomic & High-End Kitchens

A kitchen is the heart of a home, and at Square Interio, we design kitchens that combine functional efficiency with spectacular aesthetics. Our modular kitchens are custom-built to optimize space, utilizing state-of-the-art pull-out organizers, corner accessories, tall units, and smart soft-close mechanisms.

We understand the local needs in Patna, Bihar, and customize each kitchen layout—whether it's an L-Shape, U-Shape, Straight, or Parallel layout. We use 100% Boiling Water Resistant (BWR) and Boiling Water Proof (BWP) commercial marine plywood to resist moisture, and high-gloss acrylic or premium scratch-resistant laminate finishes for longevity.
