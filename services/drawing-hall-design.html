Drawing Hall & Living Space Interior

Making First Impressions Premium

The drawing room is the primary face of your home—it's where you welcome guests and spend memorable evenings. At Square Interio, we specialize in high-end drawing halls and living rooms designed to exude warmth, comfort, and luxury.

We design bespoke partition walls, luxury false ceilings, wooden paneling overlays, and custom metal trim borders. Our design layouts focus on seamless transitions between drawing and dining spaces, prioritizing premium seating arrangements, warm light placement, and optimized space planning.

Key Elements of Our Drawing Room Designs

Wall Panelling

Wood charcoal panels, marble laminates, and champagne gold metal strips that add premium texture to walls.

Smart Partitions

Elegant CNC cutting partition jaalis, brass partitions, or fluted glass blocks that divide sections without blocking light.

Ambient Ceilings

Custom gyproc ceiling contours, magnetic track lights, recessed profile LEDs, and luxury chandeliers.
